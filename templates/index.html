Galvanized allows everybody to use their unique skills to join the fight.

Sex trafficking is not a new problem. We are aware of the statistics. For years, brave social entrepreneurs have led the fight in combatting this atrocity. With the influx of volunteers and organizations, the gap has grown between those who want to dedicate their lives to the fight and those who have funds to donate to the fight. The Galvanized Project was created to help the people in the middle -- those who want to give more than money but are not inclined to create a new organization.
